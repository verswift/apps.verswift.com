About

This web app is a convenient way to make bookmarks for sites you use very often. If you have a cluttered bookmark folder then your important bookmarks get lost in that long list. This makes the bookmarking less convenient to the point you might not use them. Click a bookmark card's icon to open the link and click the trash can to delete it. The bookmark cards are saved in your browser.
